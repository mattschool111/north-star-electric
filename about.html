Our Story

Building trust through excellence in electrical services since 2016

Our Journey

Founded in 2016, North Star Electric emerged from a vision to provide exceptional electrical services to the Chatham-Kent and Windsor regions. What started as a small team of dedicated electricians has grown into a trusted name in residential electrical services.

Our founders brought over 15 years of experience in the electrical industry when they established North Star Electric. Their commitment to quality workmanship and customer satisfaction has been the cornerstone of our success.

Today, we're proud to serve our community with a team of licensed professionals who share the same dedication to excellence and safety.
